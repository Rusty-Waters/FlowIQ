# Privacy Policy for FlowIQ Instruments

**Effective date:** June 22, 2026

FlowIQ Instruments is designed as a field tool for HVAC testing, adjusting, and balancing work. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

## Information We Collect

FlowIQ Instruments does not require an account and does not collect personal information for advertising, analytics, or user profiling.

The app may process the following information on your device:

- Field measurements, calculations, saved readings, favorites, and recent tool selections entered or created in the app.
- Bluetooth Low Energy scan and connection data used to find and communicate with compatible field instruments.
- Files you choose to import, such as pump curve images or PDFs.
- Reports, CSV text, or graph files generated by the app when you choose to share, email, or upload them.

## Permissions

The app may request Bluetooth permissions to scan for and connect to compatible meters and instruments.

On some Android versions, location permission may be required by the operating system for Bluetooth scanning. FlowIQ Instruments does not use Bluetooth scanning to determine or track your physical location.

The app may also use file access through the system file picker when you choose to import a file, and internet access when you choose to upload a report to a URL you provide.

## How Information Is Used

Information handled by the app is used to:

- Perform HVAC field calculations and display results.
- Store app preferences such as favorites on your device.
- Connect to compatible Bluetooth instruments when you request it.
- Create reports or exported files when you choose to share or upload them.

## Sharing and Uploading

FlowIQ Instruments only shares reports or files when you choose a sharing action, such as email, the Android share sheet, or a cloud upload URL.

When you share information through another app or service, that third party's privacy policy and data handling practices apply.

If you enter a cloud upload URL, the app sends the selected report data to that URL. You are responsible for choosing a trusted destination.

## Data Storage and Retention

App preferences and saved app state are stored locally on your device.

You can remove locally stored app data by clearing the app's storage in your device settings or uninstalling the app.

Reports or files you share outside the app are retained according to the policies of the destination you selected.

## Children's Privacy

FlowIQ Instruments is intended for professional field use and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
